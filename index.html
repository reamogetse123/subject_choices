Grade 9 is an important year in the life of every South African learner. You need to start thinking about your careers during Grade 9. Your choice will depend on what subjects are offered at your school from Grade 10, for you to make a right choice of subjects in Grade 10. Learners should know the career path they wish to pursue on completion in Grade 12. In order to obtain the National Senior Certificate (NSC) in Grade 12 ,learners must have SEVEN subjects four that are compulsory and three which are chosen at the end of Grade 9 for Grade 10-12.
